team akatsuki

The Akatsuki was an organization that compromised of some of the most powerful rogue ninjas in Naruto. Led by several leaders over the years, they were focused on mainly during the reign of Nagato Uzumaki, an orphan from Amegakure. Under him, the members of the Akatsuki were divided into two-man teams, with each team being handled missions appropriate to them.

about akatsuki

The Akatsuki is one of the most well-known anime organizations. It was founded by Yahiko, Nagato, and Konan to promote tranquility because they didn't want other people to suffer as they did. Unfortunately, fate wasn't on their side. The group suffered a major setback when Hanzo demanded that Yahiko kill himself. The ninja wanted his dreams to come true, so he walked toward the kunai that was being held by Nagato. This particular incident changed the organization forever. It was turned into a haven for ruffians who would help Obito achievehis goal. Each member of the Akatsuki was blessed with powerful abilities, which made them a threat to every ninja.
